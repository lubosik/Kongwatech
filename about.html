About

Founder

Lubosi Kongwa

Lubosi Kongwa is a 20-year-old entrepreneur from Zambia, based in the UK.

Over the last several years, he has built AI systems, automation pipelines, outreach engines, and revenue infrastructure for real businesses across multiple industries.

Not in theory. For actual clients, with actual results.

Kongwa & Co. is where all of that experience lives. And where it is now available to select businesses.

He does not consult from a distance. He gets in the room. He builds alongside you. He owns the outcome as much as you do.

That is what makes this firm different.

A generational business.

Kongwa and Co. is not a startup. Not a hustle.
